Choose Your Track

Three ways to innovate. One goal — Build for Madhya Pradesh

Track 1: CODE IT

Hackathon — Software / Apps / AI

Build any digital solution that makes life better for MP's citizens, farmers, students, or cities.

    What You can built:

  • • Mobile apps & web platforms
  • • AI/ML solutions
  • • Chatbots & automation tools
  • • Data dashboards
  • • IoT software

    Themes:

  • • Smart Citizen (Governance,Schemes,Services)
  • • Smart City (Traffic, Waste, Water, Safety)
  • • Smart Farmer (Mandi prices, Crop advisory)
  • • Smart Student (EdTech, Career, Skills)
  • • Smart Tourism (Heritage, AR Guides)

    • Who Should Join: CS/IT students, developers, data scientists, UI/UX designers, tech startups

Track 2: BUILD IT

Makeathon — Hardware / Devices / Prototypes

Create any device or prototype that solves a real problem for MP's farms, homes, or cities.

    What You Can Build:

  • • IoT devices & sensors
  • • Robotics & drones
  • • Agricultural tools
  • • Clean energy solutions
  • • Medical devices

    Themes:

  • • Farm Devices (Soil sensors, Smart irrigation)
  • • Clean Energy (Solar, Biogas, Efficiency)
  • • Smart City Devices (Air quality, Smart bins)
  • • Health & Safety (Diagnostics, Wearables)
  • • Artisan Tools (For ODOP producers)
  • • Open Innovation (Any hardware solution!)

    • Who Should Join: ECE/Mechanical/Electrical students, makers, hardware startups, IoT enthusiasts

Track 3: SHIP IT

Export / ODOP — Business / Market-Ready Solutions

Develop any solution that helps MP's products, businesses, or artisans reach more markets.

    What You Can Build:

  • • E-commerce platforms
  • • Fintech solutions
  • • Supply chain tools
  • • Branding & packaging solutions
  • • Export readiness tools

    Themes:

  • • ODOP Global (Take Chanderi, Bagh Print worldwide)
  • • Fintech for MSMEs (Payments, Credit, Insurance)
  • • Supply Chain (Logistics, Cold chain, Tracking)
  • • Quality & Trust (Traceability, GI protection)
  • • Market Access (B2B platforms, GeM listing)
  • • Open Innovation (Any business solution!)

    • Who Should Join: MBA students, commerce graduates, fintech startups, MSME entrepreneurs, exporters, ODOP producers
